5 Do's and Don'ts for responsible social media use as a college student.

DOs
1. Maintain a professional profile with a reasonable name and depict a current photo of yourself. 
2. Consider your post before you post it and if it would be acceptable for a professor or employer to read it. 
3. Create strong passwords & enable two-factor authentication
4. Regularly review & update your privacy settings.
5. Report all instances of harassment, fake account(s) & suspicious message(s).   
When sharing another person's work and/or ideas, please give them proper credit. 

DON'Ts 
1. Do not share personal information (phone number, address, Aadhaar) with anyone outside of your immediate circle of friends or family;  
2. Do not post any offensive, hateful, or discriminatory posts/coarse language to your social media accounts, whatever platform. 
3. Do not accept any request for friendship from anyone that does not personally know you. 
4. Do not disseminate frivolous and/or unverifiable messages, complaints or grievances regarding your college, professors, and/or classmates on your social media account(s), whatever platform. 
5.Do not post during examination(s); you show future prospective employers a lack of time management skills.
